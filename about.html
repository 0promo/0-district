THE STORY

We Built What We Needed.

0PromoRecords started from one frustrating truth: African music is transforming the world — but the infrastructure behind it still hasn't caught up. Artists were signing bad deals because there was nothing else. Labels were operating on spreadsheets and group chats. Curators with world-class taste had no platform built for them.

We didn't build another playlist or another distribution pipeline. We built a District — a full-stack infrastructure for the African music ecosystem. The tools, the network, the editorial arm, and the platform, all in one signal.

No hype. No middlemen. Just music, moving on its own terms.

/ THE BACKSTORY

Born in
the Gap.

0PromoRecords started with a simple observation: the best music in the world was coming out of Africa, and the worst infrastructure in the music industry was available to the people making it.

Artists were signing rights away to move twenty copies. Labels were operating on WhatsApp groups and good faith. Curators with ears built for the next era had nowhere to put them. The signal was real. The system wasn't ready for it.

So we built the system. Starting from Accra, reaching every city the sound has touched — Lagos, Johannesburg, London, New York, Paris, Nairobi. 0 District is the infrastructure African music deserved from the beginning.

The Gap.

African music has always been world-class. The infrastructure around it hasn't been.

Our mission is to arm every artist, label, and curator on the continent — and in the diaspora — with the tools that were built for everyone else first. Upload, distribute, collaborate, curate, and earn. On your terms. Under your name. At scale.

/ 02 · VISION

African Music.
Global Standard.
No Exception.

We are building toward a world where the names that define the next era of global music are African — and they got there without giving anything away.

Afrobeats. Amapiano. Highlife. Afro Soul. Afro House. Drill. Electronic. The sounds are already everywhere. The infrastructure catches up through 0 District.

THE CREATOR HOLDS THE KEY.

/ 03 · DRIVE

The Problem
Is The Fuel.

Artists who signed their masters away for a feature that never came. Curators with world-class ears and no platform to prove it. Labels running rosters off spreadsheets and hope. We have seen it, close up, for years.

Anger is not the word. The word is clarity. You see the gap clearly enough, you stop waiting for someone else to fill it. You build.

Every feature in 0 District exists because someone needed it and it didn't exist. We build as the signal demands. The work is never done — and that's the point.

WE BUILD AS THE SIGNAL DEMANDS.

/ 04 · BELIEVE

First Principles.
No Compromise.

African music has arrived.It is not emerging, upcoming, or next. It is here. The industry needs to catch up — and we are not slowing down to let it.

Independence is a strategy.Not a limitation, not a consolation prize. The creator who owns their masters owns their future. Full stop.

The network is the release.Who hears your music matters as much as the music itself. 0 District is how you build both at once.

Curation is a discipline.Great music needs great editors to move at scale. The ear is as important as the catalog.

The District is for Operators.Artists. Labels. Curators. Every person on this platform is here to do the work — and the platform is built to back them up.
