PRIVACY POLICY

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide to us, including your email address, name, and account credentials. When you use 0 District, we may automatically collect usage information including which tracks you play, your interactions with the platform, and your device information.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve the 0 District platform. This includes creating and managing your account, personalizing your experience, sending service communications, and analyzing platform usage to enhance our services.

3. DATA PROCESSING & SECURITY

Your data is processed by 0PromoRecords and stored securely. We use Supabase as our data processor and backend infrastructure. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. DATA SHARING & SALES

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with external advertisers or marketers. Your information is only shared with service providers necessary to operate 0 District, and only under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. YOUR RIGHTS & REQUESTS

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights or file a privacy concern, contact us at privacy@0promorecords.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

6. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use cookies and local storage to remember your preferences and session state. These are essential for platform functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect your experience on 0 District.

7. POLICY UPDATES

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Material changes will be posted on this page, and if required by law, we will notify users of significant modifications.
